What is AI Artistry?

Artificial intelligence art is any artwork, particularly images and musical compositions, created through the use of artificial intelligence (AI) programs, such as text-to-image models and musical generators. It is sometimes confused with digital art.

Note:Cons: Lack of human touch: AI generated art may lack the personal touch and emotional depth that comes with being created by a human artist. Ethical concerns: Some people are concerned about the ethical implications of using AI to create art, particularly if it results in the displacement of human artists.
